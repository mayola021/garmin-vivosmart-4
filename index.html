Why Update Your Garmin Vivosmart 4?

The Garmin Vivosmart 4 is a stylish fitness tracker that offers advanced health monitoring, including Pulse Ox, stress tracking, sleep analysis, and more. To keep it running at its best, it's essential to regularly update the device’s firmware. Updates bring enhancements in functionality, bug fixes, and sometimes entirely new features, ensuring your tracker stays efficient and accurate.

Whether you're tracking your heart rate throughout the day or monitoring sleep patterns overnight, updates can help improve the overall accuracy and responsiveness of the device. They also ensure compatibility with the latest smartphone software and apps like Garmin Connect.

Benefits of Updating Your Vivosmart 4

  • Improved Battery Life: Updates often include optimizations to extend usage between charges.
  • Increased Accuracy: Enhancements to heart rate monitoring, step tracking, and Pulse Ox measurements.
  • New Features: Garmin occasionally rolls out new tools like Body Battery improvements or widget customizations.
  • Fewer Syncing Issues: Firmware fixes connectivity bugs with Garmin Connect or third-party fitness apps.
  • Security Enhancements: Updates keep your data safer and the system more stable.

Before You Start the Update

To ensure a smooth update experience, please follow these tips:

  • Charge your Vivosmart 4 to at least 50% battery.
  • Ensure the Garmin Connect app is up to date on your smartphone.
  • Have a stable Bluetooth connection between the device and your phone.
  • Keep both devices nearby and avoid interruptions during the update.

How to Update Garmin Vivosmart 4

Using Garmin Connect Mobile (Wireless)

  1. Open the Garmin Connect app on your phone.
  2. Ensure your Vivosmart 4 is paired via Bluetooth.
  3. If an update is available, the app will notify you automatically.
  4. Tap the update prompt and follow the on-screen instructions.
  5. Do not turn off or move the devices until the update is complete.

Using Garmin Express (Computer Method)

  1. Download and install Garmin Express on your computer.
  2. Connect your Vivosmart 4 using the USB charging cable.
  3. Launch Garmin Express and add your device.
  4. The software will check for and prompt you to install any updates.
  5. Follow on-screen instructions and do not disconnect until finished.

Common Issues and Troubleshooting

  • Bluetooth not connecting: Restart both your phone and Vivosmart 4, then re-pair.
  • Update not showing: Ensure Garmin Connect or Garmin Express is updated to the latest version.
  • Device freezing: Perform a soft reset by holding the button for 10–15 seconds.
